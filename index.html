Case Study: Winning the 181 2nd Avenue Listing

73,587 SF Office | Downtown San Mateo | Lincoln Properties

The Challenge

Competitive pitch against multiple brokerages for a 73,587 SF Class B+ office property. Ownership group (Mrs. Rosanna Gaw) required institutional-quality materials and a data-driven leasing strategy. Short timeline to differentiate.

The Genspark Solution

Team used Genspark to build: (1) Interactive leasing analytics dashboard with occupancy tracking, tenant pipeline, and competitive benchmarking across 12 comparable properties. (2) Transportation commute analysis mapping 5 Bay Area origins with drive times, Caltrain schedules, and talent pool data. (3) Market positioning analysis comparing 181 2nd Ave's parking ratio, rate positioning, and vacancy against the full Downtown San Mateo competitive set.

The Result

CBRE selected as exclusive listing agent. Ownership cited the quality and depth of our analytical materials as a key differentiator. The deliverables we built in Genspark are now being used as the active leasing toolkit for the property.

The 181 2nd Ave Leasing Dashboard, built in Genspark, is now the property's primary marketing and analytics tool.
