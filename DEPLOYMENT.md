### Method 1: GitHub Pages (Recommended)

**Step 1: Create GitHub Account (if needed)**
- Go to [github.com](https://github.com) and sign up
- Verify your email

**Step 2: Create New Repository**
- Click the **+** icon in top right â†’ **New repository**
- Repository name: `cbre-genspark` (or any name)
- Make it **Public**
- Click **Create repository**

**Step 3: Upload Files**
- On the next page, click **uploading an existing file**
- Drag `index.html` and `README.md` from your computer
- Scroll down and click **Commit changes**

**Step 4: Enable GitHub Pages**
- Click **Settings** (top menu)
- Click **Pages** (left sidebar)
- Under "Branch", select `main` 
- Click **Save**
- Wait 2-3 minutes

**Step 5: Get Your Link**
- Refresh the Pages settings page
- You'll see: "Your site is live at `https://[username].github.io/cbre-genspark/`"
- **That's your public link!**
